Introduction

PhD student and holder of a Master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), I leverage my skills in my thesis, which focuses on the integration of multimodality in immersive environments.

By relying on semantic web technologies, I aim to represent immersive environments, whether their content or context, to make them more understandable by machines, thus facilitating the integration of richer and more natural interactions with users.
